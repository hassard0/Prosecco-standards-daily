## Latest Summary
Acumos AI is an open-source platform and framework designed to simplify the development, management, and sharing of AI applications and Machine Learning (ML) models. It provides a standardized infrastructure for data scientists to package their models into portable, containerized microservices (using technologies like Docker and Kubernetes) that can be easily deployed across different cloud environments or on-premise hardware.

Managed by the **LF AI & Data Foundation**, Acumos aims to lower the barrier to entry for AI innovation by creating a "marketplace" for AI assets. This ecosystem allows users to discover, reuse, and chain together models to create complex AI workflows. The platform supports multiple popular toolkits and languages, including TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit-Learn, and R, ensuring that developers can use their preferred environments while benefiting from a consistent deployment and integration layer. Key features include a visual design studio for building complex models, automated microservice generation, and robust cataloging capabilities for model discovery and collaboration.

## What's New
Recent activities around Acumos AI have centered on its integration within the broader **LF AI & Data Foundation** ecosystem, emphasizing cross-project collaboration with other open-source AI tools. Development efforts have shifted toward optimizing containerized deployments and improving the 'Design Studio' for easier model chaining. The project has also been focusing on the **Acumos Marketplace**, enhancing features for federated model sharing which allows different organizations to connect their private catalogs to a public exchange while maintaining security and governance policies. Recent community meetings have highlighted maintenance updates to ensure compatibility with the latest versions of Kubernetes and cloud-native graduation standards.
